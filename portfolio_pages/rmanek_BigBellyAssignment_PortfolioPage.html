Big Belly Assignment

Summary

This assignment focused on defining a problem statement for the solar-powered garbage compactors found around Boston University. Photos of the issue can be seen on the right (Photo Credit - Professor Gutierrez).


The current situation is that during the winter, snow builds up on top of the garbage cans - this limits the amount of solar energy absorbed - consequently reducing the efficiency of the compactor within the garbage can.


A better solution to this could include having heating elements on the surface of the garbage can. This would limit the amount of snow build up, and reduce the efficiency loss of the compactor.


This solution can be visualised in the sketches seen below.

Solution

An enhanced design includes heating elements on the can's top - in order to limit snow accumulation.
